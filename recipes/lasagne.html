Lasagne Recipe

Home

Description

A classic lasagna is a baked casserole made by layering flat pasta sheets with a savory meat sauce (ragù), a creamy dairy layer, and melted cheese. It takes about 2 to 2.5 hours to make and bake

Ingredients

  • Pasta Sheets (4 to 6)
  • Bechamel Sauce
  • Grated Cheese
  • Parmesan
  • Beef Mince
  • Tinned Tomatoes
  • Tomato Paste
  • Crushed Garlic
  • Basil
  • Onion
  • Oregano

Steps

  1. Simmer the Sauce: Sauté the Onion, herbs and meats, add the tomatoes, and let the mixture simmer on low for 30 to 60+ minutes to develop deep, rich flavors.
  2. Prepare the Pasta: Boil your lasagna sheets al dente, or use fresh or no-boil sheets to save time.
  3. Layer the Dish: In a 9x13-inch pan, start with a thin layer of meat sauce. Add a layer of noodles, followed by your cream/ricotta layer, meat sauce, and cheeses. Repeat this sequence 3 to 4 times, finishing with a heavy layer of cheese on top.
  4. Bake & Rest: Cover with foil and bake at 180°C (350°F) for 30-40 minutes. Remove the foil and bake for another 15-20 minutes until the top is bubbling and browned. Let it rest for 10-15 minutes before slicing.
